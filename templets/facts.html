Facts

Our curricula embraces the need to open up students to theoretical, practical and current-market exposure. Our students get to walk this journey with our dedicated faculty leading the way.

Studing at Sherri Cupples Exodus Junior School.

Our approach focuses on Holistic Development to nurture our students into graduates ready for the next level.

Our commitment to providing Quality Education is founded on Academic Excellence, Innovation, Research, and Talent Development.

Our management, faculty, and staff are united by a mission to ensure our students receive top-notch service throughout their educational journey.

We encourages critical thinking and problem-solving through experiential learning.

Student life at Sherri Cupples Exodus Junior School.

As a student, you will have countless opportunities for personal development. All we require is your initiative to continuously evolve. We will guide you in realizing and maximizing your potential.

Student-led clubs and societies create safe and creative spaces for dialogue, debates, research, talent development, social movements, and fun. This fosters a strong sense of belonging and unity within our community.

Our students discover who society is and, most importantly, your role in making our world a better place. Personal life skills are identified, learned, and nurtured. .

Home of Sports Champions.

We believe everyone is born with talent, and we are here to nurture yours.

The sports family within the school community instills resilience, tenacity, and team spirit in all players. These activities serve as key factors in providing the student experience necessary for nurturing holistically developed students.

Our existence is rooted in our community's belief in education and a brighter future for themselves and our country.
